# Assessments

This section details the various assessment components designed to evaluate your understanding and practical application of the course material. These assessments are structured to reinforce learning and provide opportunities to demonstrate proficiency in key areas of Physical AI and humanoid robotics.

## Projects

*   **ROS 2 package development project**: A project focused on developing and implementing custom ROS 2 packages for specific robot functionalities.
*   **Gazebo simulation implementation**: Practical assignments involving the setup, configuration, and simulation of robots and environments within Gazebo.
*   **Isaac-based perception pipeline**: A project demonstrating the use of NVIDIA Isaac platform for building and evaluating AI-powered perception pipelines.
*   **Capstone: Simulated humanoid robot with conversational AI**: The culminating project where you will integrate various course concepts to create a simulated humanoid robot capable of understanding and responding to conversational commands.
